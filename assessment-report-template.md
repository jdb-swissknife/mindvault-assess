**CRM Systems**
- GoHighLevel — Best for: Trades, has built-in automation
- HubSpot — Best for: Professional services, scales well
- Pipedrive — Best for: Sales teams, visual pipeline

**Voice Agents**
- Synthflow — Best for: No-code custom agents
- Vapi — Best for: Developers, full control
- Bland.ai — Best for: Fast deployment

**Scheduling**
- Calendly — Industry standard
- SavvyCal — Best UX, calendar overlay
- Schedulicity — Best for service businesses with teams

**Email Management**
- SaneBox — Intelligent filtering, easy adoption
- Superhuman — Speed-focused, expensive
- Front — Team shared inbox

**Automation Platforms**
- Zapier — Easiest, most integrations
- Make.com (Integromat) — Visual builder, more powerful
- n8n — Self-hosted, open source
