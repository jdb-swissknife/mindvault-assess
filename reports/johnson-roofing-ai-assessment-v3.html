Recommended Solutions

Quick Wins

1

Automated Lead Response

CRM Automation
Low complexity 2-3 hrs setup

Pain: Leads come in by phone, cell, and office staff. Manual entry means delays, missed information, and slower follow-up. Industry data shows 5-minute response vs. 30+ minutes means a 21x difference in conversion.

What we build: An automated intake system connected to your Go High Level CRM. When a lead comes in, the system captures it automatically, qualifies it, scores it, and triggers follow-up tasks without anyone having to remember to enter it.

Why this fits: You already have GHL. This makes it work the way it should.

3-4 hrs/week returned in manual entry and missed follow-up
Ongoing: Managed by MindVault
2

Field Information Access

Mobile Operations
Low complexity 2-3 hrs setup

Pain: Your crew leads are on job sites all day. When they need job details, they call the office manager. When the office manager needs an update, they call you. The information exists in GHL but is not accessible in the field.

"There would be a nice thing to have in the field. On an iPad, something like that, to be able to pull up reports on where things stand."

What we build: A field-ready information layer connected to your existing GHL data. Crew leads can pull up job details, customer history, schedule, and status from their phones without calling anyone. We also set up a photo upload flow so job site photos go directly into the CRM.

Why this fits: You already have GHL mobile. You just are not using it yet.

5-7 hrs/week returned in phone calls and coordination
Ongoing: Managed by MindVault
3

Cross-Department Communication Flow

Team Coordination
Low-Medium complexity 3-4 hrs setup

Pain: Sales does not know what operations is working on. Operations does not know what is coming from sales. You are currently the communication router for the entire company.

"What I would like is more integration through the whole company so that people know how many leads are getting warm from the sales team. So the operations team can understand what stage that is at."

What we build: An automated handoff system. When a deal closes in GHL, operations gets notified. When a job is scheduled, sales gets an update. When a milestone happens, the right people know. We also set up an automated weekly summary so you have a clear picture of the business every Monday morning.

Why this fits: You already have Slack and GHL. This makes them talk to each other.

4-5 hrs/week returned in coordination calls and manual updates
Ongoing: Managed by MindVault
