Three Quick Wins

1

Lead Response Automation

CRM Integration
Low Complexity 1 Week

New leads often experience delays or incomplete information capture when your office staff take calls and manually enter details later.

What we build: We configure Go High Level to automatically trigger SMS and email responses the moment a lead enters your system. All inbound calls, form submissions, and direct inquiries get instant acknowledgment. Lead data flows seamlessly without manual re-entry.

Why this fits: You already pay for GHL. This unlocks features you are not using yet. No new tools needed.

Time returned: 3-5 hours/week

Ongoing: Managed by MindVault. We monitor response rates and tune messaging based on what converts.

2

Field Mobile CRM Access

Mobile Integration
Low Complexity 2 Weeks

Your field teams call the office manager to get job details, creating delays and interruptions.

What we build: We implement GHL's mobile app across your crew leads and field supervisors. Job details, photos, change orders, and customer information become available on iPads in the field. Digital signatures for completed work happen on-site.

Why this fits: You mentioned wanting iPad access for field teams. GHL mobile enables this without building custom software.

Time returned: 5-7 hours/week

Ongoing: Managed by MindVault. We train new staff, troubleshoot access issues, and optimize field workflows.

3

Slack Company Communication

Team Coordination
Medium Complexity 2-3 Weeks

Slack adoption failed because only you use it. Departments communicate through phone calls and ad-hoc questions.

What we build: We set up structured Slack channels for Sales, Operations, and Office coordination. GHL notifications feed directly into relevant channels, so everyone sees lead status and job progress without asking. Your crew leads and sales team get real-time updates where they already work.

Why this fits: You want integration across the company so people know what stage leads are at without phone calls.

Time returned: 4-5 hours/week

Ongoing: Managed by MindVault. Channel management, integration monitoring, and adoption coaching.
