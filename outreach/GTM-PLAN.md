## SECTOR-SPECIFIC PAIN POINTS TO HIT

### HVAC
- Seasonal demand spikes → scheduling chaos in summer/winter
- Emergency calls at 2 AM → no one answering, leads go to competitors
- Quote follow-ups → 80% of quotes never get follow-up call
- Technician routing → driving back and forth across Twin Cities
- Parts inventory → guessing what to stock

### Electrical
- Project quoting → too much time estimating, not enough billing
- Permit management → paperwork bottleneck
- Seasonal slowdowns → need to maximize revenue in busy months
- Customer communication → "when is the electrician coming?"

### Roofing
- Lead qualification → tire-kickers vs real jobs
- Insurance claim workflows → massive paperwork
- Crew scheduling → weather-dependent, high rescheduling
- Material ordering → over/under ordering costs thousands
