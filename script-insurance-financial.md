# Insurance / Financial Services AI Assessment Script
## Industry-Specific Fork (Post-Identification)

Trigger: When prospect identifies as insurance agent, financial advisor, mortgage broker, or related

---

## MODIFIED SECTION 1: INSURANCE/FINANCIAL OVERVIEW

**Q1 (Replace): Business identity**
"Tell me about your practice—are you focused on personal lines, commercial, Medicare, or a mix? And are you captive with one carrier or independent?"

*Listen for: P&C vs life/health vs Medicare, captive vs independent, niche specialization*

**Q2 (Replace): Client base and book size**
"How many clients are you actively managing? And what's your team setup—do you have CSRs, or is it just you handling everything?"

*Listen for: Book size, service capacity, renewal season stress, admin burden*

**Q3 (Replace): Production focus**
"What percentage of your time is spent on new business vs servicing existing clients? And how many new policies are you writing per month?"

*Listen for: Service trap vs growth focus, cross-sell opportunity, renewal retention*

---

## MODIFIED SECTION 3: INSURANCE/FINANCIAL LEAD & REVENUE LEAKS

**Q8 (Replace): Speed-to-quote**
"When someone requests a quote—how quickly can they get numbers? And how much of that process is manual on your end?"

*Listen for: Delayed quotes, manual data entry, lost opportunities to faster agents*

**Q9 (Replace): Renewal management**
"How do you handle renewals? Are you reviewing every policy proactively or just reacting when clients call?"

*Listen for: Reactive renewals, missed cross-sell opportunities, retention risk*

**Q10 (Replace): Client communication preferences**
"How are you communicating with clients today—phone calls, emails, text? And are you able to meet them where they prefer?"

*Listen for: Generic communication, no preference tracking, wasted follow-up attempts*

**Q11 (Replace): Quote-to-bind follow-up**
"What happens after you give someone a quote? How do you stay in touch until they bind or decline?"

*Listen for: No nurture sequence, quotes forgotten, bind rate analysis missing*

**Q12 (Add): Referral requests**
"Are you systematically asking clients for referrals, or does it happen only when you remember?"

*Listen for: No referral system, missed organic growth opportunities*

**Q13 (Add): Compliance and documentation**
"How are you handling E&O documentation, coverage confirmations, and compliance requirements?"

*Listen for: Manual documentation, compliance risk, E&O exposure*

## INSURANCE/FINANCIAL QUICK WINS

### Quick Win 1: Instant Quote Response System
**Problem:** Prospects request quotes, get busy response, agent loses them to faster competitor
**Solution:** Automated acknowledgment with AI chatbot gathering info + instant scheduling
**Tools:** GoHighLevel, Quoteburst integration, or custom
**ROI:** Response time <2 min = 7x more likely to bind; capture quotes that used to ghost
**Setup:** $800-$1,500 initial, $100-200/month

### Quick Win 2: Renewal Review Automation
**Problem:** Renewals handled reactively, cross-sell opportunities missed, retention risk
**Solution:** Automated renewal review scheduling 60 days out with AI-prepopulated comparison
**Tools:** GoHighLevel, Better Agency, or AgencyZoom
**ROI:** 5-10% improvement in retention; $15K-$50K protected annual revenue per 1% retention
**Setup:** $1,000-$2,000 initial

### Quick Win 3: Referral Campaign System
**Problem:** Referrals only when remembered, no systematic request process
**Solution:** Automated referral request post-bind, plus referral partner nurture
**Tools:** GoHighLevel campaigns, or Agency Revolution
**ROI:** 20-30% increase in referral volume; lowest cost of acquisition
**Setup:** $600-$1,200 initial

### Quick Win 4: Client Preference Hub
**Problem:** Calling clients who prefer text, emailing clients who never check email
**Solution:** Preference capture at binding + automated channel-specific communication
**Tools:** GoHighLevel custom fields + conditional logic
**ROI:** 40% higher engagement rates; less wasted outreach effort
**Setup:** $400-$800 initial

## INSURANCE/FINANCIAL KPIs TO CALCULATE

1. **Quote-to-Bind Rate**
   - Quotes given monthly
   - Current bind rate
   - Industry benchmark (20-40%)
   - Revenue lost to quotes not followed up

2. **Retention Rate Cost**
   - Current retention %
   - Cost of 1% retention loss (typically $5K-$15K per point)
   - Revenue protected by proactive renewals

3. **Cross-Sell Gap**
   - Policies per client currently
   - Target (3-5 policies per client)
   - Commission value of closing the gap

4. **Referral Pipeline Value**
   - Current referrals/month
   - Potential with systematic process
   - Annual value of referral growth

5. **Service Call Burden**
   - Service requests per week
   - Time per request
   - Cost of service vs revenue-generating activities

## INSURANCE/FINANCIAL TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS

**Agency Management Systems:**

**Better Agency** - Best for: Independent agents wanting modern platform
- Pros: Built for insurance, marketing automation included, clean UI
- Cons: Smaller ecosystem, newer platform

**AgencyZoom** - Best for: Captive agents, activity tracking
- Pros: Activity management, goal tracking, good for teams
- Cons: Less robust marketing features

**HawkSoft** - Best for: Established agencies, detailed accounting
- Pros: Strong accounting/commission tracking, mature platform
- Cons: Older interface, steeper learning curve

**Applied Epic** - Best for: Large agencies, enterprise needs
- Pros: Industry standard, extensive integrations
- Cons: Expensive, complex, overkill for small agencies

**Quote/Bind Integrations:**
- PL Rating systems (EzLynx, PL Rater)
- Life quoting (Quoteburst, iPipeline)
- Medicare (Sunfire, Connecture)
